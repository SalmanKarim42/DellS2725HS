DELL_S2725HS is Not Running

You have connected successfully to your DELL_S2725HS device, but the DELL_S2725HS web application doesn't seem to be running.

If you continue to see this message, please try the following troubleshooting steps:

Solution 1: Restart the DELL_S2725HS service

If you previously enabled SSH on your DELL_S2725HS, you can check whether the DELL_S2725HS service is running by logging in via SSH and running the following command: 

sudo service DellS2725HS status

If the service is not listed as Active: active (running), restart the DELL_S2725HS service with the following command: 

sudo service DellS2725HS restart

After the command completes, reload this page.

Solution 2: Restart your DELL_S2725HS

If you previously enabled SSH on your DELL_S2725HS, log in via SSH and try restarting your DELL_S2725HS by running the following command: 

sudo reboot

If you don't have SSH access, wait two minutes before powering off your DELL_S2725HS. Then, power on your DELL_S2725HS, and wait a minute before reloading this page.

Solution 3: Reset your DELL_S2725HS to factory settings

If you have just updated your DELL_S2725HS, or if your DELL_S2725HS recently experienced sudden power loss, your DELL_S2725HS may have a corrupted filesystem.

A factory reset will restore your DELL_S2725HS back to a working state.
